Questions, answered

How long does a simulation take a candidate?+
Most scenarios run between 45 and 90 minutes and candidates complete them on their own schedule, so there is no live coordination overhead for your team.
Do simulations work for non-technical roles?+
Yes. fydell covers analytical, operational, commercial, and leadership roles. Any job where judgment and real work matter can be simulated.
How do you keep scoring fair?+
Every candidate is measured against the same validated rubric for the role. Scoring is structured and auditable, which removes most of the bias of unstructured interviews.
Can we build our own scenarios?+
On Team and Enterprise plans you can design custom scenarios with your own tasks, tools, and rubrics, with support from our team.
How does fydell handle data and privacy?+
Candidate data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise plans add SSO, custom roles, and configurable retention to match your policies.
