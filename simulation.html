Risk assessment

Decide which risks are most likely to break the thesis, and whether they are mitigable in the deal terms.

  • Customer concentration. Top 5 customers are 41% of revenue; the largest is 16%.
  • Competitive pressure. Two national carriers are expanding into Solstice's core regions.
  • Wage inflation. Driver costs are rising faster than pricing power.
  • Key-person risk. The founder-CEO intends to exit 12 months post-close.
  • Execution risk. The 17% margin target depends on automation not yet deployed at scale.
A strong answer names the two or three risks that matter most and says how you would mitigate them, not just lists all five.

Final recommendation

Write your recommendation to the investment committee. State your call (buy, hold, or pass), the price you would pay, your read on valuation, the key risks, and whether the strategic fit justifies the premium.
